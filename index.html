Rewards that work!

When employees complete courses, they not only enhance their knowledge but also earn additional rewards. These incentives encourage further learning, leading to continuous knowledge growth.

Coins

The number of coins earned is based on course length and quiz results. These can be used to pick up gift cards and exciting merchandise at Quala's own rewards store.

Tickets

Similar to coins, the tickets earned are based on course length and quiz performance. Employees use these tickets for draws, offering chances to win awesome prizes. Draws happen regularly, featuring seasonally updated rewards.

Stronger Together

Quala enhances the partnership between suppliers and retail chains for product training, ensuring store employees gain deep product insights. This direct knowledge transfer from suppliers boosts employees' capacity to deliver informed and impactful customer service.

In Quala's reward system, gaining knowledge leads to tangible rewards. Completing courses earns employees coins and lottery tickets, with amounts based on course length and quiz outcomes.
